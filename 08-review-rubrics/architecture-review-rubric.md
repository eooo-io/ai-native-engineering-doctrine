# Architecture Review Rubric

## Questions

- What problem is this design solving?
- What constraints matter most?
- What are the main components and boundaries?
- Who owns the data?
- What can fail?
- How is the system observed?
- How is it deployed and rolled back?
- What alternatives were rejected and why?
- What is the expected scaling path?
- What security assumptions exist?

## Output

Use risks, trade-offs, and recommended next steps rather than vague approval language.
