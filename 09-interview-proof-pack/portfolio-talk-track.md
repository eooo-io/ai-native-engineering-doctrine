# Portfolio Talk Track

## Short version

I maintain a portable AI-native engineering standards system that turns architecture principles, coding conventions, security rules, and review policies into tool-specific instructions for coding agents and human teams.

## Longer version

Modern AI-assisted development needs more than prompts. It needs boundaries, standards, review policies, test expectations, and architecture rules that can be reused across tools and projects.

This harness is my public-safe scaffold for that operating model. It can be adapted to Codex, Claude, Cursor, Copilot, and repo-local `AGENTS.md` files while keeping the durable engineering doctrine tool-neutral.

## Interview emphasis

- I bring reusable standards, not just experience.
- I use AI to accelerate disciplined engineering, not to bypass it.
- I can onboard teams and agents into consistent conventions.
- I think in systems: code, architecture, delivery, security, and operations.
